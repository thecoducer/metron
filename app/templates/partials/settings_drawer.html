Sync Broker Accounts

Zerodha

Connect your Kite Connect API accounts. Credentials are stored securely and never displayed.

1
Sign up on Zerodha Developer Portal

Go to developers.kite.trade and sign up for a new account.

Important: The developer portal login is separate from your Zerodha Kite account. You must create a new account on the developer portal first, even if you already have a Zerodha trading account.

When prompted to choose a plan, select the Free plan.

2
Create a new app

Click Create new app. Fill in:

  • App name: anything (e.g. "Metron")
  • Redirect URL (web app):
    https://metron.thecoducer.com/api/callback
  • Postback URL: leave blank
3
Copy your credentials

After creation, copy the API Key and API Secret from the app details page.

4
Paste them here

Click Add Account below, enter a label, paste your key & secret, and hit Save.

5
Authorize & sync

Click Login next to the account — authorize on Zerodha's page and your portfolio loads automatically.

Import from CAS PDF

Upload your CAMS/KFintech Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) PDF to auto-import mutual fund holdings and transaction history.

1
Go to CAMS website

Visit camsonline.com CAS page.

2
Select statement type

Choose Detailed statement type. Select Specific Period and pick a wide date range (e.g. Jan 2020 to today) to get full transaction history. In Folio Listing Type, choose With Zero Balance Folios to include redeemed funds.

3
Enter your details

Enter your registered email and select CAMS + KFintech as the RTA. Submit the form.

4
Download the PDF

CAMS takes a few minutes to generate and send the PDF to your email. Open it with the password you set on the CAMS website when generating the statement.

Your PDF is processed in memory and never stored on our servers. The password is used only to unlock the PDF — it is not your account password. This is the password you set while generating the statement on the CAMS website.

What would you like to import?

If you've already added these funds to your portfolio via a broker API or manually, importing them again here would create duplicate holdings. In that case, choose Transaction history only — you still get the full buy & sell log without affecting your portfolio numbers. If you haven't added them anywhere else, select both.
