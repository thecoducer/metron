Zerodha

Connect your Kite Connect API accounts. Credentials are stored securely and never displayed.

1
Sign up on Zerodha Developer Portal

Go to developers.kite.trade and sign up for a new account.

Important: The developer portal login is separate from your Zerodha Kite account. You must create a new account on the developer portal first, even if you already have a Zerodha trading account.

When prompted to choose a plan, select the Free plan.

2
Create a new app

Click Create new app. Fill in:

  • App name: anything (e.g. “Metron”)
  • Redirect URL (web app):
    https://metron.thecoducer.com/api/callback
  • Postback URL: leave blank
3
Copy your credentials

After creation, copy the API Key and API Secret from the app details page.

4
Paste them here

Click Add Account below, enter a label, paste your key & secret, and hit Save.

5
Authorize & sync

Click Login next to the account — authorize on Zerodha’s page and your portfolio loads automatically.
