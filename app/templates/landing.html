Features

Built for power users

Designed by an engineer who wanted a dashboard that actually works the way it should.

Real-time updates via SSE

Server-Sent Events push live data to your browser. No polling, no WebSocket overhead — just a persistent HTTP stream.

Multi-account Zerodha

Connect multiple Zerodha accounts. Holdings are merged and grouped by symbol across all accounts.

Smart auto-refresh

Auto-refreshes during market hours (9:00–16:30 IST). Optional 24/7 mode for after-hours tracking.

Google Sheets as database

Physical gold and FD data lives in a Google Sheet auto-created in your Drive. You own the data, always.

Dark mode & privacy mode

Toggle dark theme and privacy mode (blurs sensitive data) from the user menu. Persists across sessions.

Responsive & minimal

Clean UI with Inter font, horizontal-scroll tables on mobile, compact Lakhs/Crores number formatting.

Privacy

Your data, your rules

The app runs in the cloud. Your financial data doesn't. It stays in your Google Drive — the developer has zero access to it.

Data stays in your Google Drive

All your financial data — gold holdings, fixed deposits — lives in a Google Sheet in your Drive. The app reads and writes to it, but the data is yours. You can view, edit, or delete it anytime.

Developer has no access

The app developer cannot see your portfolio, holdings, or any financial data. Your Google Sheet is private to your account. Period.

Open source — verify it yourself

The entire codebase is on GitHub. Review the source code yourself, or paste it into ChatGPT / Claude and ask it to run a safety and anti-malicious check. Nothing is hidden.

Encrypted sessions

Zerodha access tokens are encrypted. Credentials are never stored — only short-lived session tokens that expire automatically.

Revoke anytime

Remove Google access from your account permissions page. It's instant.
