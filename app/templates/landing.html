How does encryption protect you?

What if Firebase gets breached?

An attacker with the entire Firebase database and the server secret still cannot read your broker credentials. They'd also need your 6-character PIN — which is never stored anywhere. Not in the database, not on the server, not in logs. It exists only in your head.

Brute-force protection

Three wrong PINs and you're locked out for 15 minutes. Keep guessing and that climbs to 1 hour, then 4. The rate limiter runs server-side, so clearing your browser won't save you.

100% open source. Read it yourself.

Every line of code is on GitHub. Don't take our word for it — read the code, or paste it into ChatGPT / Claude and ask "is this safe?" Open source is the only honest security policy.
