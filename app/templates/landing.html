Features

Built for power users

No bloat, no gimmicks — just a fast, reliable dashboard built the way it should be.

Real-time streaming

Live data pushed to your browser via Server-Sent Events. No polling, no reload — just a persistent stream that updates as prices move.

Multi-account support

Connect multiple broker accounts. Holdings are merged and grouped by symbol across all linked accounts.

Smart auto-refresh

Refreshes automatically during market hours (9:00–16:30 IST). Switch to 24/7 mode for after-hours tracking.

Your Drive is the database

All investment data lives in a Google Sheet auto-created in your Drive. No proprietary databases — you own and control the data.

Dark mode & privacy mode

Toggle dark theme and privacy mode (blurs sensitive data) from the user menu. Persists across sessions.

Mobile-ready & minimal

Clean UI that works on any screen. Horizontal-scroll tables on mobile, compact Lakhs/Crores formatting throughout.

Privacy

Your data, your rules

Metron runs in the cloud. Your financial data doesn't. It stays in your Google Drive — always.

Data stays in your Google Drive

Every piece of financial data lives in a Google Sheet in your Drive. The app reads and writes to it, but you own it. View, edit, or delete it anytime.

Developer has no access

The app developer cannot see your portfolio, holdings, or any financial data. Your Google Sheet is private to your account. Period.

Open source — verify it yourself

The entire codebase is on GitHub. Review the source code yourself, or paste it into ChatGPT / Claude and ask it to run a safety and anti-malicious check. Nothing is hidden.

Encrypted sessions

Broker access tokens are encrypted. Credentials are never stored — only short-lived session tokens that expire automatically.

Revoke anytime

Remove Google access from your account permissions page. It's instant.
